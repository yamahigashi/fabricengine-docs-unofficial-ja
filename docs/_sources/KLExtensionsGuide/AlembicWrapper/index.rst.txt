AlembicWrapper Extension
====================================================================================

.. image:: /images/FE_logo_345_60.*
   :width: 345px
   :height: 60px

| |FABRIC_PRODUCT_NAME| version |FABRIC_VERSION|
| |FABRIC_COPYRIGHT|

The :ref:`alembicwrapper_extension` provides a high level wrapping for the :ref:`alembic_extension`. Even though this layer introduces abstraction, the low level alembic objects such as the :kl-ref:`AlembicIObject` or :kl-ref:`AlembicOObject` can still be accessed. The main objects of this extension are the :kl-ref:`AlembicArchiveReader` and the :kl-ref:`AlembicArchiveWriter`.
