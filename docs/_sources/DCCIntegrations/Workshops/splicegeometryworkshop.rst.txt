Description
--------------------------
This workshop covers the topics of creating procedural geometry in Maya, and continues to describe how tools can be made portable with Splice, moving the procedural geometry from Maya to Softimage, Arnold and Nuke. This workshop targets users with experience in geometry generation, 3D Math and geometry pipeline issues (such as procedural geometry in an offline renderer).

You can find all of the videos embedded below in the `Fabric Engine Workshop Channel on Vimeo <https://vimeo.com/channels/629477>`_.

`All workshop videos are available for download as well. <http://dist.fabric-engine.com/Downloads/Tutorial-Videos.rar>`_
